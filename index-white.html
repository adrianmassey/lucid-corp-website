FAQ

Questions, answered.

What makes Lucid Infinity™ different from a traditional tray?

Lucid Infinity™ is a two-chamber, pad-less design. Purge drains away from the product into a sealed lower chamber — so there's no absorbent soaker pad, no glue, and no contamination. The result is better merchandising, longer shelf life, and a tray that's fully recyclable.

Is the tray really 100% recyclable?

Yes. The tray is made entirely from clear PET with no fabrics, pads, or desiccant beads bonded to it. That means it goes straight back into the PET recycling stream — from bottle, to tray, to bottle again.

Do I still need a soaker pad?

No. The lower chamber manages purge on its own, eliminating the soaker pad entirely. Independent testing showed up to 50,000× less bacteria versus pad-based trays at 32 hours.

What products is it suited for?

Protein, berries, cut fruit, and high-volume food service. The design is proven across blind taste tests and freeze-thaw performance, and the tray can be tailored to a range of product formats.

Can the trays be customized?

Yes. With in-house design, tooling, and engineering under one roof, we develop custom molds and formats quickly — matching your product, branding, and merchandising needs.
