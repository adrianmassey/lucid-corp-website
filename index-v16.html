Why Lucid Corp

Built on three core principles.

Every decision we make — material, form, performance — is guided by the same three commitments that have driven Lucid Corp from day one.

01

Superior Presentation

The tray is the first thing the consumer sees. We design every detail — geometry, material clarity, labeling surface — to make your product the hero at the point of purchase.

02

Freshness Engineering

From barrier materials to insert drainage geometry, every component is selected for its role in the freshness system — minimizing waste and maximizing the quality window for retailers and consumers.

03

Circular by Design

We engineer for end-of-life from the very first sketch. Our trays are made from a single recyclable material family — no mixed-plastic lamination, no separation required at the recycling stream.
