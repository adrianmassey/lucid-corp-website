Capabilities

What we bring
to the shelf.

Full capabilities deck →
01
Modular Tray System
One platform, every fresh food category. The Lucid Infinity™ system adapts across produce, protein, bakery, and food service with interchangeable inserts and lids.
02
Retail Presentation
Engineered to maximise shelf impact. Clean lines, premium materials, and precision-formed geometry that elevates the product and drives purchase.
03
Sustainable Design
Built with sustainability at its core. Recyclable materials, reduced plastic weight, and a circular design philosophy aligned with retailer commitments.
04
Custom Configuration
Tailored to your SKU, your category, your retailer. From tray depth to insert geometry, every element is configurable to your specification.
05
Supply Chain Ready
Designed for the full cold chain. Robust, stackable, and sized for standard racking — reducing damage, improving logistics, and cutting waste.
06
Brand Integration
Your brand, elevated. Custom colour, finish, and print options allow the Lucid Infinity™ system to become a seamless part of your brand identity.
